About Zed Tuition,Inc.

We are a developed, focused technology company creating accessible digital education platform.

Our Story

Zed Tuition started with a simple mission: make quality educational content easily accessible to everyone. It is now becoming public to every country. What began as a passion project has grown into two fully operational platforms used by learners and readers around the world.

We believe in building lightweight, fast, and user‑friendly Education website that serve real needs — whether it’s a student looking for chemistry notes or a professional tracking global documents.
